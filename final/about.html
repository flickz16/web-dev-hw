About Me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Zachary Flickinger and I'm a senior at University of Montana majoring in Journalism and minoring in Arabic Studies. My other passion is photography, specifically portraits and I'm currently shooting with a Canon 60d.

I was raised in Columbia Falls almost my entire life and I visit the Flathead Valley often to see my family. The love of my life is a brown-eyed pyrador named Aspen (you saw her on the gallery page.) My other passions are playing the piano, basketball, traveling, profesional wrestling and most importantly the Denver Bronocos!
